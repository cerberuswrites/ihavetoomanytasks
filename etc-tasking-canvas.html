ON: THREAD STYLES

vignettes

suitable for 3+ PARTICIPANTS. see relevant SCENE NOTES.
essentially, a set of interconnected scenes that interact with each other either on-screen or off-screen, playing out in a loose chronological order.

e.g. CHAR A & CHAR B post 1 time each, capturing a moment during the "leadup" of an investigation. CHAR C & D follow up with a 3rd and 4th post where they raid and search an area, having reconvened with A & B off-screen in-between the two scenes.

character dev

development-style post within a group setting. focused on a character, activity, etc. likely minimal interaction, but posts within the thread are all connected through a predetermined theme or setting.

traditional turn order

the usual play by post. u know the drill.

round robin

suitable for 3+ PARTICIPANTS. round-based event format, with updates every ~72 hours from a DM. no set turn/posting order.
